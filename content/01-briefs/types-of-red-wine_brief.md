Reader wants a clear, scannable guide to all major red wine styles — body weight, taste profile, key regions, and food pairings. They may be picking a bottle for dinner, trying to understand a wine menu, or figuring out which reds they actually like. Secondary intent: beginner-friendly but not condescending.

## Voice Notes

- Organise by body weight (light → medium → full) — consistent, scannable, maps to how readers shop
- Name real dishes for food pairings (not "red meat" — "a slow-braised lamb shoulder")
- Explain tannins in the flow, not in a glossary box
- No em-dashes. No "not X, just Y" patterns.
- Intro must not lead with what wine IS — lead with what it DOES for the reader

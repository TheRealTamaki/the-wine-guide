## Search Intent

Reader is a wine-curious adult who hasn't locked in preferences yet. They've had a few glasses, maybe found one they liked, and they want a short-list of approachable red wines to try. They're intimidated by tannin, by snobbery, and by the word "dry." They want wines that taste good immediately without needing a steak to make sense.

## Wine Lineup Plan

**8 reds**, all beginner-friendly, priced $12–$25. Focus on:
- Lower-tannin, fruit-forward styles (Pinot Noir, Beaujolais, Merlot)
- Approachable New World fruit (Malbec, Zinfandel, Red Blend)
- Off-dry / slightly sweet styles (one lighter Italian or Lambrusco)
- Easy food-friendly reds (Chianti, Rioja Crianza)

**Selection criteria:**
- Under $25
- Customer rating 4.0+ where available
- Low to medium tannin (no Brunello-style tannin bombs)
- Known brand or producer the beginner might recognize

## Search Intent

Reader knows they drink white wine but wants to understand the landscape: what the main varieties are, how they differ, and how to pick the right one for a meal or occasion. They're not total beginners (they know "Sauvignon Blanc" exists) but they can't reliably tell a Viognier from a Pinot Grigio blind. They want confidence, not a textbook.

Primary intent: informational/navigational — "give me an overview of white wine varieties I can actually use."

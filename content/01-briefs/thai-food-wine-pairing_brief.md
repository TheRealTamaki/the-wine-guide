## Reader intent
Someone with Thai takeout on the way (or a reservation booked) who wants to know what bottle to pour. They're not picking a sommelier course. They want a fast, confident answer for pad thai, green curry, tom yum, larb, etc., and a rule they can use forever.

## Quick answer
Off-dry Riesling is the safest single bottle for Thai food. Sweetness softens chili heat, acidity carries fish sauce, lime, and lemongrass without flinching. Gewurztraminer, dry Riesling, sparkling wine, and dry rose all do similar work. Save big tannic reds for another night.

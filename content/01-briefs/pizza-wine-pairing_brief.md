## Quick answer (TLDR)

A medium-bodied Italian red with bright acidity is the safest single bottle. Sangiovese (Chianti), Barbera, or Montepulciano cover most tomato-based pizzas. White pizza wants Chardonnay or Pinot Grigio. Spicy or sweet toppings (Hawaiian, BBQ chicken) push you toward Lambrusco, Zinfandel, or off-dry Riesling.

## Search intent

Curious drinker who wants approachable whites. Pinot Grigio / Sauvignon Blanc / off-dry Riesling / Moscato territory. They want "I know I like this" bottles, not complex Burgundian Chardonnay.

## Lineup plan (8 wines, $12–$28)

- Pinot Grigio (beginner staple)
- Sauvignon Blanc — Marlborough (bright and easy)
- Sauvignon Blanc — New Zealand alternative / Loire
- Off-dry Riesling (the sweet-but-balanced gateway)
- Chardonnay (unoaked or light-oak only, no oak bombs)
- Moscato or Gewürztraminer (sweet, floral, confident beginner pick)
- Vinho Verde or Albariño (light, food-friendly)
- Prosecco or sparkling (dry-sweet range)

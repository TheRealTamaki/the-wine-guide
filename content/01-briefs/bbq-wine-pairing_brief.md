## Core question
What wines pair best with BBQ — across ribs, brisket, pulled pork, grilled chicken, burgers, sausages, plus smoked vs grilled and sweet vs spicy sauces?

## Quick answer (TLDR)
For most BBQ, reach for fruit-forward, slightly smoky reds with soft tannin. Zinfandel, Malbec, Syrah/Shiraz, and Grenache cover most of the table. Lighter dishes (chicken, sausages, fish) want rosé, Riesling, or chilled Pinot Noir. The sauce decides more than the meat: sweet sauce wants ripe fruit, spicy sauce wants off-dry whites.

## Hook angle
The reader has stood at the bottle shop on a Saturday with brisket on the smoker and no idea whether to grab Cabernet or something fruitier. Solve the meat-plus-sauce decision quickly.

# Brief: Best Wine Advent Calendar

**Target keyword:** best wine advent calendar
**Category:** gift-guides
**Date:** 2026-04-26

---

## Search Intent

Gift buyer, November to December window, shopping for a wine-loving partner, family member, or friend. They want the classic advent calendar concept — one door per day, one bottle of wine — but they don't know where to buy one or which brands are worth the money.

Secondary intent: gift buyers who've heard about Aldi and Costco wine advent calendars and want to know if they're actually good (they are, but they sell out fast).

---

## Key Research Finding — Amazon Limitation

Actual wine advent calendars (24 mini bottles, one per day) are **not sold on Amazon US** due to alcohol shipping and licensing restrictions. This affects the article structure:

- Primary picks: Direct-from-retailer wine advent calendars (Aldi, Costco, Virgin Wines, Laithwaites, Vinebox). No AmazonCTA for these.
- Amazon picks: One or two wine gift sets that can be presented as "buy-now alternatives if you need something shipped immediately." AmazonCTA for these only.

The article acknowledges this upfront and positions itself as the honest guide that tells you where these actually sell.

---

## Proposed Picks (8 picks across 3 tiers)

### Budget (under $40)
1. **Aldi Wine Advent Calendar** — the cult favourite. 24 mini 187ml bottles. Sells out in October. ~$35. In-store only, no shipping.
2. **Costco Wine Advent Calendar** — 24 bottles, mix of red/white/rosé from global regions. ~$40. In-store pick-up, some markets available online.

### Mid-tier ($80–$130)
3. **Vinebox 12 Nights of Wine** — 12 full-pour tubes of hand-selected wines from Europe. Ships US-wide. ~$90–110.
4. **Wine Insiders Wine Advent Calendar** — 24 full 187ml bottles, curated from California and international producers. Ships US-wide. ~$120.
5. **Bev Advent Calendar** — 24-can sparkling rosé calendar (250ml cans). Ships US-wide. ~$99. Best for rosé and sparkling fans.

### Premium ($150+)
6. **Laithwaites Wine Advent Calendar** — 24 full-size 750ml bottles. Ships from UK, available to select US states. ~$200–250.
7. **Virgin Wines Advent Calendar** — 24 full-size 750ml bottles hand-picked by MW sommeliers. Ships from UK. ~$180–220.
8. **Flaviar/Winc Premium 12-Bottle Curated Set** — club-based, customisable, 12 full bottles. ~$150+. Amazon alternative pick.

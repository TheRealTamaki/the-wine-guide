## Angle

Chicken is the flexible protein that changes personality based on the sauce. The core rule: pair the wine with the sauce and cooking method before the meat. Roast chicken is flexible, lemon chicken wants acidity, creamy chicken wants Chardonnay or Chenin Blanc, grilled chicken can handle lighter reds, fried chicken wants bubbles, and barbecue chicken wants fruit.

## Core Pairing Guidance

- Roast chicken: Chardonnay, Pinot Noir, Chenin Blanc, Cotes du Rhone, dry rose.
- Grilled chicken: Chardonnay, Sauvignon Blanc, Pinot Noir, Grenache, Beaujolais.
- Fried chicken: Champagne, Cava, Cremant, dry Prosecco, sparkling rose.
- Lemon chicken: Sauvignon Blanc, Vermentino, Albarino, Pinot Grigio.
- Creamy chicken: Chardonnay, white Burgundy, Chenin Blanc, Viognier with restraint.
- Barbecue chicken: Zinfandel, Grenache, Shiraz, Lambrusco, dry rose.
- Spicy chicken: off-dry Riesling, Gewurztraminer, sparkling wine, low-alcohol rose.
- Chicken curry: Riesling, Gewurztraminer, Chenin Blanc, sparkling wine.
- Chicken pasta: pair by sauce, not protein.

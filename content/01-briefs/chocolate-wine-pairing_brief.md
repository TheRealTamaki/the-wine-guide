## Reader question
"What wine actually goes with chocolate? I always reach for a big red and it tastes weird. What's the rule?"

## Quick answer
The wine should always be at least as sweet as the chocolate. Dark chocolate (70%+) loves Port, Banyuls, Maury, and bold reds like Zinfandel. Milk chocolate wants softer reds (Pinot Noir, Merlot) or sweet pinks like Brachetto d'Acqui. White chocolate wants Moscato d'Asti, Sauternes, or a late-harvest Riesling. Match weight and sweetness, and the pairing snaps into place.

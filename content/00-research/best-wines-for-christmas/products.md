## Lineup overview

21 verified candidates across five Christmas wine styles: sparkling (4), red (7), white (2), rosé (3), port/dessert (3). The set spans the full range of Christmas occasions: aperitif, gift, dinner centrepiece, dessert pairing. Price range $16.97 (Matsu El Picaro, Mionetto Prosecco) to $39.99 (La Rioja Alta Ardanza). No guessed scores. No guessed IDs.

The sparkling bucket covers four distinct angles: accessible Prosecco (La Marca), value Prosecco (Mionetto), the Christmas sweet-red-sparkling tradition (Banfi Rosa Regale Brachetto), and a premium DOCG Prosecco Superiore (Adami). The red bucket runs from light Chianti and Rhône blends up through Spanish Rioja, Malbec, and California Cab — enough variety that Lia can bucket by body weight. The Port bucket covers both ruby (Graham's, Warre's) and tawny (Sandeman 10yr) styles, since cheese-and-Port is as Christmas as it gets. Two Provence rosés (Miraval, Whispering Angel) are included alongside the budget Chateau Montaud — Lia can pick one or two depending on wine count.

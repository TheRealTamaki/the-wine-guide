# SEO — References

Last updated: 2026-04-13

## SEO Targets

- **Primary focus:** Long-tail educational wine keywords (lower competition, higher intent)
- **Content types:** Guide articles, comparison posts, "best of" lists, regional deep-dives
- **Word count targets:** 1,500-2,500 words for guides, 800-1,200 for focused posts

## Keyword Data

- Master keyword list: `wine-site-seo-keywords.xlsx` (project root)
- Use `/dataforseo` for live volume/difficulty checks
- Use `/neuronwriter-seo` or `/beuron-seo` for SERP-based term extraction

## On-Page SEO Standards

- **Title tag:** Primary keyword near the front, under 60 characters
- **Meta description:** Include primary keyword, compelling CTA, under 155 characters
- **H1:** One per page, matches search intent
- **H2s/H3s:** Use secondary keywords naturally in subheadings
- **Images:** Alt text with descriptive, keyword-relevant descriptions
- **Internal links:** Link to related articles within the site
- **Affiliate links:** Use `rel="nofollow sponsored"` on all affiliate links

## Notes

- Wine content competes with massive authority sites — differentiate through depth, specificity, and unique angles
- Long-tail keywords like "best wine for thanksgiving dinner under $20" convert better than "best red wine"
- E-E-A-T matters in wine (experience, expertise) — content should demonstrate real knowledge

# The Best Wine Pairings for Thanksgiving Dinner

Published November 21, 2022

## Introduction

The author is hosting her first Thanksgiving and will handle both turkey roasting and wine selection. She offers guidance on choosing wines for the holiday meal, noting that the celebration involves multiple flavors including savory, salty, sweet, sometimes spicy, hearty, etc.

## What to Consider When Choosing Wine Pairings for Thanksgiving Dinner

When selecting wines for the meal, consider these factors:

- Budget
- Number of guests to serve
- Your wine palate and experience (and that of your guests)

The author emphasizes that wine selection need not be complicated, as options exist for various preferences and budgets.

## The Best Wine Pairings for Thanksgiving Dinner

### Bubbles

Sparkling wines work well with nearly any dish and create a celebratory atmosphere. They pair nicely with appetizers, dessert, or as an after-dinner drink. Recommended options include:

- J Cuvee 20
- Champagne Cattier Brut
- Domaine Carneros Brut

### Chardonnay

This white wine cuts through rich, savory dishes like turkey, stuffing, mashed potatoes, and rolls. Top selections feature:

- Macrostie Chardonnay
- Trois Noix Chardonnay
- Gary Farrell Bacigalupi Chardonnay
- Copain Sea Lift Chardonnay

### Pinot Noir

An iconic Thanksgiving pairing, Pinot Noir's bright red fruit characteristics complement traditional holiday foods and encourage relaxed conversation. Favorites include:

- Domain Anderson Pinot Noir
- Merry Edwards Pinot Noir
- Copain Maggy Hawk Pinot Noir
- Story of Soil Pinot Noir

## Additional Recommendations

The author suggests that there really are no rules when it comes to wine pairings for Thanksgiving dinner. Other varietals worth considering include cool climate Syrah, Merlot, Grenache, and Gamay (Beaujolais), all of which pair beautifully with turkey meals.

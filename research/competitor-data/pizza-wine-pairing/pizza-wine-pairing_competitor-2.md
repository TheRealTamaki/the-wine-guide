# Wine and Pizza Pairing Guide

Pizza evokes comfort, and the pairing of wine with pizza has deep roots in Italian tradition. Today's pizza varieties span the globe with diverse toppings, and wine options range equally wide. Here's guidance for matching wines to your preferred pizza style.

## Margherita Pizza (The Original Pizza)

The foundational pizza features tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and basil in simple combination.

**Pairings:** Pinot Grigio, Sauvignon Blanc, Rosé, Sangiovese, and sparkling wines.

## Pepperoni Pizza

Success with pepperoni pizza involves counterbalancing the spice, salt, and fat content of the meat topping.

**Pairings:** Zinfandel, Grenache, Syrah, and Tempranillo.

## Vegetarian Pizza

When vegetables dominate your pizza, seek wines that highlight their fresh qualities. Sauvignon Blanc and unoaked Chardonnay complement these flavors effectively.

**Pairings:** Sauvignon Blanc, Rosé, sparkling wines, unoaked Chardonnay.

## Meat Lover's Pizza

This style demands bold, full-bodied wines to match its intense flavors.

**Pairings:** Malbec, Cabernet Sauvignon, Zinfandel, Barbera, Nebbiolo, and Vermentino.

## Hawaiian "Pizza"

For this polarizing style, contrast works best. Riesling and Gewurztraminer balance the ham's saltiness while enhancing the pineapple's acidity.

**Pairings:** Demi-sec sparkling wines, Riesling, and Gewurztraminer.

## Bottom Line

No rigid rules govern pizza and wine pairing. Enjoyment matters most. Choose what appeals to you personally.

# A Quick Guide to the Best Pizza and Wine Pairings

Published June 22nd, 2021 by DeNicola's Italian Restaurant

## Overview

Pizza and wine are beloved by millions of Americans. The article notes that "98% of Americans eat pizza" while approximately three-quarters of adults consume wine. Combining these two preferences creates an opportunity for sophisticated dining experiences.

## Best Wine for Pizza

When selecting Italian wine to accompany pizza, consider these versatile options:

- Sangiovese
- Nero d'Avola
- Fiano
- Vermentino
- Barbera

For specific toppings, more targeted pairings enhance the overall experience.

## Specific Pizza and Wine Pairings

**Pepperoni Pizza**
Sangiovese works best with pepperoni. The wine's acidity complements the topping's fat and salt content while balancing its smokiness.

**Meat Toppings**
Cabernet Sauvignon pairs well with pizza featuring non-pepperoni meat toppings. The wine's tannins offset the natural fats in meat.

**Vegetarian Pizza**
Choose Chardonnay or Sauvignon Blanc. Chardonnay's subtle citrus and herb notes complement vegetables, while dry rosé also works well.

**Hawaiian Pizza**
Riesling or Prosecco are ideal choices. Avoid heavy red wines with intense fruit flavors, as these overpower pineapple's taste.

**White Pizza**
Pair ricotta-based pizzas with crisp white wines, champagne, Pinot Grigio, or earthy Sauvignon Blancs.

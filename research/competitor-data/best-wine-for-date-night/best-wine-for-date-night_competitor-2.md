# How to Choose the Right Wine for Date Night

Wine selection doesn't have to be stressful. The Tap on Ponce offers practical guidance for picking the perfect bottle to impress your date.

## Key Strategies

**Learn the backstory.** Every wine has a narrative, whether it's a California Chardonnay surviving wildfires or a French vintage made by the same family for centuries. Sharing these details creates meaningful conversation.

**Ask your date directly.** There's no shame in inquiring about their preferences. "Don't put a ton of pressure on yourself to magically know what kind of wine your date likes." Remembering a wine they've previously mentioned earns bonus points.

## Red Wine Options

**Sweet varieties** like Syrah or Shiraz pair wonderfully with hearty foods such as steak and cheese.

**Dry reds** offer darker fruit notes such as cherry, plum, raspberry, and sometimes earthy undertones. Merlot is recommended as a versatile, food-friendly choice.

## White Wine Options

White wines suit novice drinkers well, offering more forgiving, crowd-pleasing profiles.

**Sweet whites** complement Asian cuisines including Thai, Indian, and Vietnamese dishes by balancing spice and complexity.

**Dry whites** provide versatility across menus, pairing well with seafood, poultry, and pork.

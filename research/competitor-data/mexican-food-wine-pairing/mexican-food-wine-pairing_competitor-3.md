# The Best 4 Taco Wine Pairings (with Recipes!) 2023

May 05, 2023 | By Coravin

## Introduction

Tacos and tequila aren't the only pairing options for Mexican cuisine. While Mexico is renowned for tequila and mezcal, its diverse culinary landscape offers exciting wine-pairing opportunities. From migas and carne adobada to tamales and enchiladas, Mexican dishes showcase unique and varied flavors.

Mexico produces numerous wine varietals, with Cabernet Sauvignon being the most common, followed by Carignan, Merlot, Chenin Blanc, and Muscat Blanc. These wines work wonderfully with classic taco recipes.

## Four Taco and Wine Pairing Recommendations

- **Salmon tacos + Pinot Noir**
- **Marinated steak (or black bean) tacos + Malbec**
- **Ground beef tacos + Tempranillo**
- **Carnitas tacos + Rhône-style white blend**

## Blackened Salmon Tacos

Pairing blackened salmon with Pinot Noir works because the fruity, earthy notes complement the fish while acidity cuts through its richness.

**Ingredients:**
- 1½ pounds boneless salmon filets
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp soy sauce or coconut aminos
- ¼ tsp black pepper
- 1 Tbsp olive oil
- 6-8 small corn or flour tortillas

**Mango Salsa:**
- 2 medium mangos, diced
- 1 large avocado, diced
- ½ small red onion, minced
- 1 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
- 1 lime, juiced
- 1 jalapeño, cored and minced (optional)

## Marinated Steak Tacos

These tacos feature chimichurri sauce paired with bold, citrusy Malbec.

**Chimichurri Ingredients:**
- 4 garlic cloves, peeled
- ½ cup fresh parsley
- ½ cup fresh cilantro
- 1 cup olive or avocado oil
- 1 lemon, juiced
- 1 Tbsp dried oregano
- 1 Tbsp crushed red pepper
- 1 tsp salt

Marinate 1½ pounds of flank steak in half the chimichurri using a ziplock bag for 1 hour. Cook to preference and assemble tacos with corn tortillas, cooked steak, chopped avocado, and remaining chimichurri.

## Ground Beef Tacos

This Americanized taco uses ground beef cooked with standard taco seasoning. Top corn or flour tortillas with cooked beef, shredded iceberg lettuce, chopped tomato, shredded cheddar, and sour cream. Tempranillo, a medium to full-bodied wine with acidity and high tannins, complements the beef and spices well.

## Carnitas Tacos

This slow-cooker recipe pairs beautifully with Rhône-style white wine.

**Ingredients:**
- 2½ pounds pork shoulder, cut into 2 to 4-inch chunks
- 1 lime, juiced
- 2 tsp salt
- 2 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 4 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 yellow onion, diced

# Winter Wine Ideas

Published: Jan 22, 2021 | Categories: Michigan Wine, Winery

Now that everyone has had a couple of weeks to settle into the new year, we thought it was a perfect time to give you a little new year, new me energy boost. Whether it is trying a new vintage, new varietal, or just a new way to enjoy sipping on our favorites, nothing energizes and excites the Black Star team quite like wine. We have compiled a list of some fun wine-related activities that will hopefully inspire you and maybe have you excited to try something new.

## Outdoor Winter Party

Since Black Star Farms is located in Northern Michigan, it is almost a requirement for residents and visitors alike to find ways to enjoy the great outdoors, yes, even in winter. If one of your goals for the new year was to get outside more but you are having a hard time finding the motivation to brave the cold, here is the perfect solution.

Call up your social group and invite them over for a gathering in your own backyard. Outdoor barbecues do not have to be reserved strictly for summer, so suit up in your warmest winter gear, start up the grill, light a bonfire, and pour a glass of your favorite wine. The team recommends warming up with hearty red wines like their Vintners Select or Cabernet Franc, or with steaming mulled Cherry Wine prepared over the fire.

If you cannot host a bonfire, stop by either of the Traverse City area tasting rooms for a glass of wine around one of their fire pits.

## Savasana and Sip

After the holiday season, many people have resolved to better their physical health. Yoga and wine is a natural pairing. As yogi Becky Kalajian explains: There is a natural link between yoga and wine. Both support your immune system and make you feel great.

One of the most significant benefits of yoga is how it brings your mind and body into sync with one another and opens and heightens all the senses. After a good yoga session, your mind, nose, and taste buds are perfectly primed to taste wine and truly soak in all the flavors and aromas.

Practice savasana and sipping by trying a virtual yoga class, then enjoying wine in the comfort of your living room. The team recommends their 2019 Dry Riesling, which offers intense aromatics and bright fruit characteristics.

## Movie Night

With all the great shows and movies hitting streaming platforms recently, get out the comfy pillows, tasty snacks, and of course, wine.

Try these pairings:
- Barrel Aged Chardonnay with buttered popcorn
- Bedazzled or Sparkling Riesling with potato chips

If you have kids at home, pick them up a bottle of sparkling juice to enjoy while you sip on your wine. Black Star Farms offers sparkling raspberry and white grape juice available for purchase in their tasting rooms.

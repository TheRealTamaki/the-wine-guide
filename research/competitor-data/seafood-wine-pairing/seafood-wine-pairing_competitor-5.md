# Pairing Wine with Seafood

## What Seafood Goes Well with Wine

Selecting seafood to enjoy with wine doesn't require extensive expertise. The article outlines several pairings:

- **Prawns or Shrimps**: Albarino, Pinot Grigio, Vinho Verde
- **Shellfish**: Alsatian Gewurztraminer, Gruner Veltliner, Sauvignon Blanc, Vouvray
- **Salmon**: Merlot, Pinot Noir
- **Mackerel**: Moscophilero, Pinot Noir
- **Mussels**: Muscadet
- **Cold Lobster**: Albarino, Gewurztraminer, Sancerre, Vintage
- **Grilled Lobster**: Chardonnay, Meursault

## What Wines Go Well with Seafood

The guide identifies top wine selections and their recommended seafood pairings:

- **Albarino and Verdelho**: Clams, crabs, lobsters, mussels, shellfish
- **Chenin Blanc, Italian Vermentino, Greek Assyrtiko, Sauvignon Blanc, Pinot Grigio**: Lobsters, raw clams, oysters, shrimps, white fish
- **Dry Fino Sherry**: Cooked shrimps
- **Chardonnay, Italian Grillo, Viognier, Pinot Gris**: Bluefish, mackerel
- **Gamay, Grenache, Pinot Noir, Sangiovese**: Bluefish, marlin, mackerel, salmon, swordfish, tuna

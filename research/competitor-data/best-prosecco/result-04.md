# The Best Prosecco Bottles To Gift For A Sparkling Holiday

By [Keyla Vasconcellos](https://www.forbes.com/sites/keylavasconcellos/ "https://www.forbes.com/sites/keylavasconcellos/"),

Former Contributor.

Forbes contributors publish independent expert analyses and insights.

getty

The holidays are coming fast, and Prosecco makes an easy, crowd-pleasing gift when you choose the right style. Most people in the U.S. still grab Extra Dry, even though it’s the sweeter version. Brut actually means dry, so if you want a Prosecco that isn’t sweet, choose Brut or Extra Brut instead of Extra Dry. Gifting one of these styles is the simplest way to change someone’s mind about Prosecco this season.

Prosecco has grown past its “spritz mixer” reputation, and producers across the region are pushing for more expressive, more thoughtful bottles. Like Champagne, the name Prosecco is protected and earned only when the production rules and geography line up. Even Prosecco Rosé became part of the category in 2020, adding a vibrant, newer option that lands well with anyone who likes something festive with a little color.

Each pick below links to the producer, but your local wine shop may remain the quickest way to secure a bottle while the season is in full swing.

## [Masottina R.D.O. Brut](https://www.masottina.it/en/rdo-brut/)

PROMOTED

![Masottina R.D.O.](https://imageio.forbes.com/specials-images/imageserve/69165a4243bb375eee59e973/Masottina-R-D-O-/960x0.png?format=png&width=144)

Masottina R.D.O.

Masottina

Masottina’s R.D.O. Brut leans into finesse rather than flash. The bubbles stay tight, the feel is smooth, and the wine acts as a palate cleanser throughout the meal. It’s a strong fit for someone who skips the spritzes and prefers a bottle that keeps the food front and center.

## [Bottega Gold Prosecco DOC Spumante Brut](https://bottegausa.com/collections/prosecco-sparkling-wines/products/bottega-gold-prosecco-doc-spumante-brut)

Bottega

No Prosecco says “holiday” faster than Bottega Gold. The metallic bottle does the heavy lifting, and the wine inside is bright enough to carry the room once it’s poured. Bring this when the goal is simple: show up with something festive that always earns a spot on the table.

Most people know La Marca from the light-blue label that anchors the spritz world, but Luminore steps into a more refined lane. It tastes more focused, feels more composed, and brings a touch of luxury without pushing into a higher price bracket. Hand this to someone who already trusts the brand and wants an upgraded Prosecco moment for the holidays.

## [Ruggeri Saltèr](https://ruggeri.it/en/vini/salter/)

![Rugerri Saltèr](https://imageio.forbes.com/specials-images/imageserve/69165c3e59373570cc1e321f/Rugerri-Salt-r/960x0.png?format=png&width=144)

Rugerri Saltèr

Ruggeri

Ruggeri has a strong reputation for producing polished, reliable Prosecco, and Saltèr sits in that upper tier. It’s lively enough for a party pour and steady enough to hold attention once the main dishes hit the table. This is a smart bottle for someone who usually reaches for Champagne and wants something that still feels celebratory and well made.

## [Val d’Oca Millesimato Extra Brut](https://www.valdoca.com/en/prod_cat/classic-cuvees/)

![Val d’Oca Extra Brut](https://imageio.forbes.com/specials-images/imageserve/69165d6ab14c007524d23244/Val-d-Oca-Extra-Brut/960x0.png?format=png&width=144)

Val d’Oca Extra Brut

Val d’Oca

Val d’Oca’s Extra Brut shows how Prosecco shifts when the sweetness drops and the focus moves to clarity and freshness. The millesimato labeling highlights a single harvest, giving the wine a more defined Prosecco character compared to broader blends. It works well for anyone who prefers a drier style and wants a Prosecco that stays bright and refreshing through a long holiday meal.

## [Antonio Facchin & Figli Prosecco Spumante DOC Treviso Millesimato Brut](https://www.antoniofacchin.com/en/wines/prosecco-spumante-doc-treviso-millesimato-brut/)

Antonio Facchin puts serious intention behind their vintage-dated bottles, and this one makes that clear. The profile stays dry and crisp, which helps it stand apart from the sweeter Proseccos people usually open during holiday gatherings. It’s a dependable choice when you want something thoughtful that still stays within a reasonable price range.

## [Il Fresco Prosecco DOC Rosé Millesimato Brut](https://www.villasandi.it/en/metodo-classico-opere-trevigiane-rose-3-anni.html)

![Il Fresco Prosecco DOC Rosé Millesimato Brut](https://imageio.forbes.com/specials-images/imageserve/69165f3703eca2a6fafca956/Il-Fresco-Prosecco-DOC-Ros--Millesimato-Brut/960x0.png?format=png&width=144)

Il Fresco Prosecco DOC Rosé Millesimato Brut

Villa Sandi

Prosecco Rosé is still a relatively new addition to the category, and Villa Sandi has become one of the standouts producing it at a high level. Their estate is one of the most beautiful properties in the Prosecco region, and the winery’s long list of awards reflects the care behind this bottle. Il Fresco Prosecco DOC Rosé Millesimato Brut is a festive option that delivers a Rosé Prosecco with a current style, a celebratory feel, and the credibility of a producer that consistently wins awards.

## [Mionetto Valdobbiadene Prosecco Superiore DOCG Brut (Luxury Collection)](https://usa.mionetto.com/us/products/luxury-collection/valdobbiadene-prosecco-superiore-docg-brut)

![Mionetto Valdobbiadene Prosecco Superiore DOCG Brut (Luxury Collection) ](https://imageio.forbes.com/specials-images/imageserve/691660122589eeec38af40e0/Mionetto-Valdobbiadene-Prosecco-Superiore-DOCG-Brut--Luxury-Collection-/960x0.png?format=png&width=144)

Mionetto Valdobbiadene Prosecco Superiore DOCG Brut (Luxury Collection)

Mionetto

Mionetto is one of the most recognized Prosecco names in the U.S., but the Luxury Collection shows a side of the winery that fans may not know. The DOCG designation tells you this bottle comes from the more regulated, higher-quality zone in the Prosecco hills, where tighter rules and smaller production areas shape a more refined style. It works especially well for anyone convinced they don’t like Prosecco; just make sure to choose the Luxury Collection or a Brut rather than an Extra Dry.
